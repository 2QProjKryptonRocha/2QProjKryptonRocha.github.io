Background

Leo/need is a band consisting of Hoshino Ichika, Tenma Saki, Mochizuki Honami, and Hinomori Shiho. They were once childhood friends, but they have gone strained and distant after a certain incident in high school.
One day, Ichika found a song named "Untitled" on her phone. She played the song, and was transported to the Classroom SEKAI and meets the SEKAI's Hatsune Miku.
