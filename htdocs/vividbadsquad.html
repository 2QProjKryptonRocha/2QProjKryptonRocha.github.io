Background

Vivid BAD SQUAD is a group of street musicians Azusawa Kohane, Shiraishi An, Aoyagi Toya, and Shinonome Akito, who teamed up with their goal: to pass a legendary event known as RAD WEEKEND.
Azusawa Kohane bought a CD for her mom and got lost in Vivid Street, where she stumbles upon Shiraishi An and other musicians peforming at Weekend Garage. After Kohane comes back and performs at Weekend Garage a week after. An invited Kohane to sing and sees her potential as a singer, requesting her to be her partner. Kohane agrees, forming the duo, Vivids. On they performance, they happened to perform with another duo consisting of Aoyagi Toya and Shinonome Akito, BAD DOGS. The two duos were in a conflict which each other and eventually come across a song named "Untitled" on their phones, transporting them to the Street SEKAI.
