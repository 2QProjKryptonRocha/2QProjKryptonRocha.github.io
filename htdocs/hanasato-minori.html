BACKGROUND

Hanasato Minori is a member of the idol unit MORE MORE JUMP! She's a first-year student at Miyamasuzuka Girls Academy.


Minori was an unlucky child; one day she dropped the baton during a race at school and she stayed at home the next day out of fear that her classmates would get mad at her. She saw Kiritani Haruka from ASRUN on TV and was inspired by her words, "Tomorrow will always be a better day." She was a fan of Haruka ever since and also decided to become an idol. She had auditioned more than 50 times and to no avail, but despite this she never gives up on her dreams and continues to try to accomplish it.


APPEARANCE

Minori has shoulder-length brown hair and light gray eyes. The right side of her hair is braided and clipped with an orange and white pin.


Her unit outfit consists of a white sleeveless blouse, a white vest, a loose black with the unit's three-leaf clover motif, a white ruffled skirt, and white heeled shoes. Her vest design is similar to Haruka's and her overall unit outfit is accented in her color of pale orange.


Her school uniform consists of the standard uniform of her school: a light gray seifuku and a red ribbon, without a cardigan.


Minori normally wears a pale orange sweater with a white blouse underneath, a peach bowtie, a pastel yellow skirt with a frilly layer.


PERSONALITY

Minori is a hard-working and optimistic girl; she's very dedicated and determined to becoming an idol like Haruka. She's very kind and considerate, but also expressive and clumsy. She also often speaks what's on her mind.


ETYMOLOGY

Hanasato (花里): Hana(花) means flower; Sato(里) means village.


Minori (みのり): "Minori"

RELATIONSHIPS

The content for this section may contain story spoilers

Kiritani Haruka: An idol, a fellow unit member, and a friend. Minori wishes to be an idol like Haruka; she was also grateful for her and wants to give something to Haruka in return. Minori, along with Airi and Shizuku, encouraged Haruka to return to the idol stage and perform with them as MORE MORE JUMP!



Momoi Airi: A fellow unit member and friend. Although Airi was and is strict on Minori whenever she coaches her, Minori admires Airi's passion and experience as an idol.


Hinomori Shizuku: A fellow unit member and friend. Minori admires her beauty, grace, and hard-working nature.


Mochizuki Honami: A schoolmate and a friend. They often walk their dogs together and sometimes talk about them as well.


Azusawa Kohane: A schoolmate and a best friend. They were each other's first friend in high school and often hang out together inside and outside school.
