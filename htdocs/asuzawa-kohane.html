BACKGROUND

Azusawa Kohane is a member of the street music group Vivid BAD SQUAD. She's a first-year student at Miyamasuzuka Girls Academy.


Kohane gets lost after buying a CD for her mom, stumbling upon Vivid Street, An Shiraishi and her fellow musicians, and Weekend Garage. Amazed by An's musical abilities, she came back to Weekend Garage a week later, where An recognizes her and performs for her. An saw her potential as a musician and offered her; Kohane accepted, forming the duo Vivids. On their first day of performance, they were challenged by duo BAD DOGS, Akito and Toya. After the reconciled, they then form the street musician unit, Vivid BAD SQUAD.


APPEARANCE

Kohane has light brown hair and light brown eyes. Prior to joining Vivid BAD SQUAD, she had waist-length hair worn into two low pigtails and wore a pair of round glasses. After joining the unit, she cut her hair to shoulder-length and wears her hair to shorter pigtails. Instead of wearing glasses, she wore contact lenses.


Her unit outfit consists of a gray word with the word Smile printed on it with a white collared shirt underneath, a denim skirt, a white belt with the word "ANGEL" repeatedly printed along it, and an oversized pink, red, and-white baseball jacket. She also wears a black and white cap (often backwards), a back choker with a gold heart pendant, and gray socks with white stripes.


Her school uniform consists of the standard uniform of her school: a light gray seifuku and a red ribbon, without a cardigan.



PERSONALITY

Prior to the formation of Vivid BAD SQUAD, Kohane was a quiet, shy girl; she lacked self-confidence and didn't like being in public places. As the story progresses, she becomes more open and gains more confidence. Despite being shy, she seems to enjoy thrilling things and activities such as roller coasters, bunjee jumping; she even owns a pet snake, which An and the others are scared of.

RELATIONSHIPS

The content for this section may contain story spoilers

Shiraishi An: A fellow unit member and her singing partner. The two were extremely close, as An gave Kohane the confidence to perform onstage. An often brags and compliments Kohane, leaving Kohane flustered and bashful. Akito stated that An "only ever talks about Kohane."



Shinonome Akito: A fellow unit member. Kohane was intimidated by Akito, as Akito criticized Vivids very harshly. However, she still helped him and Toya reconcile with each other. She also respects Akito for his skill, while the latter respects the other for her talent and determination.


Aoyagi Toya: A fellow unit member. They became good friends after spending time as a group. Both of them enjoy reading as well. Kohane and Toya often tries to calm their partners down when they get into heated rivalry, an example being which duo is better.


Hinomori Shiho: A classmate and friend. Kohane had problems talking to her due to her cool impression, but they eventually bond over their love for music.


Hanasato Minori: A schoolmate, a friend, and a fellow committee member. Kohane and Minori were each other's first friend in high school. Minori inspired Kohane to chase her own dreams, seeing Minori's determination to be an idol.


Otori Emu: A schoolmate and a friend. Kohane seems to understands Emu's onomatopeic style of speaking, even to the point she was able to guide her to winning a watermelon smashing contest.
