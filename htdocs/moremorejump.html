Background

MORE MORE JUMP! is an idol group consisting of three retired idols Kiritani Haruka, Momoi Airi, Hinonomori Shizuku, and an ambitious girl who dreams to be an idol.
Young Hanasato Minori aspires to be an idol after seeing Kiritani Haruka on TV. After Haruka retired from being an idol, she met Minori, and then they stumbled upon Airi Momoi and Hinomori Shizuku having an argument. After a series of events, Airi and Shizuku decides to help Minori with reaching her dreams of becoming an idol. They stumbled upon a song named "Untitled" and was transported to the Stage SEKAI where Hatsune Miku and Kagamine Rin try to convince the three retired idols to return to their idolhood.
