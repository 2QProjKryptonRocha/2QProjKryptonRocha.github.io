BACKGROUND

Hoshino Ichika is the main vocalist and guitarist of the band unit Leo/need. She's a first-year student at Miyamasuzuka Girls Academy.


She's known Saki, Honami, and Shiho since they were kids, but a certain event has caused them to grow distant. She enjoys listening to songs sung by Hatsune Miku. She would often sing Miku's songs while playing the guitar given to her by her father.


APPEARANCE

Ichika has blue eyes and a long-black hair.


Her unit outfit consists of a black tank top underneath a white sleeveless blouse, a black skirt with a blue lining, a blue coat wrapped around her waist, a black boots with black socks. She also wears a black fingerless glove on her right hand and a couple of black mini-belts on her right thigh. Unlike her fellow bandmates, she wore her red-and-white ribbon on her right wrist.


Her school uniform consists of the standard uniform of her school: a light gray seifuku and a red ribbon, without a cardigan.


She typically wears a gray hoodie over a red and black flannel along with black pants and black and white sneakers.


PERSONALITY

While Ichika may seem like a cold, stoic person at a glance, she's a sweet caring girl who looks after her friends. She's passionate about music; she even asked Nene to teach her how to singing. She cherishes her childhood friends very deeply and she gets happier when she's with them. She loves Miku's songs and always listens to them. She dislikes noisy places and thrill rides, which is why she doesn't enjoy going to Phoenix Wonderland unless she's with Saki.


ETYMOLOGY

Hoshino (星乃): Hoshi(星) means star; No(乃) can mean "from" or "of".


Ichika (一歌): Ichi (一) means one and Ka (歌) means "song". This name comes from her parents getting together from their love of one song.

RELATIONSHIPS

The content for this section may contain story spoilers

Tenma Saki: Childhood Friends and Bandmates. Ichika enjoys Saki's joyful personality, and Saki is glad that Ichika is always there for her. Ichika visits Saki in the hospital the most often aside from her family. Ichika worries that Saki overworks herself as she tries to experience the things she was never able to when she was hospitalized.

Mochizuki Honami: Childhood friends and Bandmates. They became a little distant over the years as Honami feared that her classmates would bully Ichika, but they have since then made up.


Kiritani Haruka: Classmates from 1-C and friends since the "Run! Sports Festival! Committee Rush" event. Ichika's one of the few people who don't treat Haruka differently due to her status as an idol.


Tenma Tsukasa: Brother of a Childhood friend. Ichika thinks he's a good at waking people up.
